Immigration Exams

Immigration Medical Exams
I-693 and N-648

Paperwork ready in 4–5 business days if all results are normal.

We are a USCIS-authorized Civil Surgeon practice providing complete immigration physicals and required immunizations. We handle all necessary paperwork efficiently so you can move forward with your application with confidence.

Required Vaccines for I-693 (Age 18–65):

  • Tdap / Td
  • Polio
  • Hepatitis B
  • Varicella
  • MMR — Measles, Mumps, and Rubella
  • Influenza

MMR, Varicella, and Hep B can be checked in the blood for immunity — $75 per test or $225 for all three.

If age is under 18 or over 65 years, additional vaccines may be required.

Please note: No insurance accepted for the immigration exam itself. Insurance can be used at the lab and pharmacy for required tests and vaccines. Additionally, we will provide you with an itemized receipt that can be used to request a claim for your payments if you have Premera insurance with Microsoft or Amazon.
