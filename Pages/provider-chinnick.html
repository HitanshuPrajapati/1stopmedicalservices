About

"Through the use of evidence-based medicine and actively listening to the individual needs of patients, every person can set and reach achievable health goals."

Jamie graduated from The George Washington University Family Nurse Practitioner program with a 3.98 GPA. She believes that the proper standard of care involves shared decision making between the healthcare provider and the patient. Prior to completing her education, Jamie spent 8 years working in the nursing field.

In her spare time, she enjoys spending time with her husband and two children, going on family trips, and enjoying time outside soaking up the beautiful Western Washington scenery.
